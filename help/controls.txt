The default controls are the following:
		
Skip map/Return to main menu: [E]
Testing mode: [M] (your signature must be set to "Developer", otherwise this won't work)
Player 1 move left: [Left]
Player 1 move right: [Right]
Player 1 move up: [Up]
Player 1 move down: [Down]
Player 1 shoot left: [Delete]
Player 1 shoot right: [Page Down]
Player 2 move left: [A]
Player 2 move right: [D]
Player 2 move up: [W]
Player 2 move down: [S]
Player 2 shoot left: [C]
Player 2 shoot right: [V]
